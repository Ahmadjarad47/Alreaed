Product Description

The iMac "M1" 8-Core CPU/8-Core GPU/4 USB-C Shaped Ports (2021) model features a 5-nm Apple M1 processor with 8 cores (4 performance cores and 4 efficiency cores), an 8-core GPU, a 16-core Neural Engine, 8 GB of onboard RAM, and a 1 TB onboard SSD.

This model is housed in a sleek, ultra-thin aluminum case with a 23.5" Retina 4.5K display in a stunning two-tone Blue color. Equipped with a 1080p FaceTime HD camera, a studio-quality three-mic array, and a high-fidelity six-speaker system, it offers an immersive audio-visual experience.

Key Features and Benefits:

  • Brilliant 4.5K Retina display: A 24-inch, True Tone display that adapts to your environment, offering sharp, vivid colors and a comfortable viewing experience.
  • 1080p FaceTime HD camera: With improved resolution and a larger sensor, it captures more light for superior video quality.
  • Studio-quality mics: The three-mic array reduces background noise for crisp, clear conversations.
  • Six-speaker sound system: Creates rich, immersive sound, perfect for music, movies, and more.
  • M1 chip: Delivers up to 85% faster CPU performance and twice the graphics power.

Connectivity includes two Thunderbolt/USB 4 ports, two USB 3 ports, a 3.5mm headphone jack, Wi-Fi 6, and Bluetooth 5.0 for seamless wireless connections.

A-Grade/CR: iMacs are in 9/10 Cosmetic Condition and are 100% Fully Functional with a 90-day seller warranty. Shipped with generic packaging and accessories.
